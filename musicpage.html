THE YELLOW TAPE

The Yellow Tape was created between 2016 & 2019 during the beginning of my 6 month adventure in the City of Huntsville, TX. The tape is full of samples pulled from the songs that played on the local radio station and things seen on TV during the time. All mixed together, it presents as one track

MIRA!

MIRA! is a collab between Alexander and visual artist S. Alexander created music based on different illustrations created by S. The music of MIRA! accoumponied the pieces throughout there lifecycle including gallery installments and social media post.

Limeade

Limeade was created as a release for a lot of ideas that didnt fit into any other projects i was working on at the time. Meant to flow and be more of an example of the process of enjoying a thing you have created along side the people it was created for. Accepting your own talent and work as good and more than good. Its my favorite album to date.

Chopping Exercises

Chopping Exercises is simple, samples and drums programed to the MPC and played over and over and over and over and over and over and over and over andover and over anover anover and over and over until... you notice the words sound different. 12 Tracks of imfectious earworm music.
